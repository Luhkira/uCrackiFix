What type of devices do you repair?

We at Crack N' Fix are able to repair a wide range of phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops. Almost any brand or company will be no problem. If there is any question about whether or not your device can be repaired by our services, reach out to us through our contact page and we will get back to you as soon as possible to assist you!

If my device has multiple issues, how do you charge the customer?

In the case that a device has multiple issues but does not fall under "Full Device Restoration", we will only charge based on the most expensive problem and include the rest of the repairs under that price, regardless of how many issues the device has.

What is the difference between Crack N' Fix and other repair companies?

Crack N' Fix not only has a wider selection of repair options for a wider selection of devices, we also offer faster services and the most competative prices over other repair companies. We prould show our work off and are determined to only provide satisfaction to all customers.

How do I schedule a repair for my device?

To request a repair, head over to our "Send Us Your Device" page, and fill out the proper information for us to know. We will recieve the request within a few minutes of sending, and we will contact you back in your provided email within 24 to 48 hours. If you need to schedule within 24 hours of the request, contact us in our contact page and we will help you as soon as we can!

How long do device repairs usually take?

Each device takes a different amount of time for us to repair. Phones normally take a couple of hours to repair. Tablets take around a day or two. Laptops may take up to 5 days. Desktops may take up to or a little over a week to repair. All of these times depend on the size of the device, the severity of the issue, and the state the device is in when we recieve it.

Should I backup my data before a repair?

We always recommend for customers to backup their data before a repair is made, as a repair could potentially lead to the loss of data stored on the device. We at Crack N' Fix are not responsible for any data lost due to a repair, so a full backup of the device in question is highly recommended.
